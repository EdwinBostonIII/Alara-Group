Insurance

Insurance is being transformed by AI faster than almost any other industry. From underwriting automation to claims processing to fraud detection, the opportunities are enormous. But so are the risks if you get it wrong, both operationally and with regulators.

Our team has worked with insurers ranging from regional carriers to national players, and we understand the regulatory landscape, the legacy system challenges, and the delicate balance between efficiency and the human touch that policyholders still expect.

Where AI Creates Value in Insurance

  • Automated underwriting that speeds decisions while maintaining accuracy and fairness
  • Claims processing that reduces cycle times and improves policyholder satisfaction
  • Fraud detection that catches patterns humans miss without false positives that frustrate good customers
  • Customer service enhancement that handles routine inquiries while escalating complex issues to agents

AI implementations that satisfy state regulators and build trust with policyholders through transparency and explainability.

Defense

We have worked within defense organizations and understand how to navigate these complexities. We help defense contractors and agencies identify AI opportunities that are both technically feasible and practically implementable within operational constraints and contracting realities.

Defense AI Applications

  • Logistics and supply chain optimization across complex networks
  • Predictive maintenance for equipment, vehicles, and systems
  • Document processing, contract analysis, and compliance reporting
  • Training, simulation, and decision support enhancement

Technology

Technology companies often have the technical talent to build AI solutions, but that does not mean they have figured out how to apply AI strategically. The question is rarely "can we build this?" but rather "should we, and how does it fit our product roadmap?"

We help technology companies think through AI strategy at the business level: which features to prioritize, how to differentiate from competitors, and how to avoid the common traps that turn AI projects into expensive distractions.

Technology Company AI Priorities

  • Product AI roadmap development and prioritization
  • Build vs. buy vs. partner decisions for AI capabilities
  • AI governance and responsible AI frameworks
  • Internal process automation to improve engineering productivity

Common Tech Company Questions We Answer

Healthcare

Healthcare is where AI's potential to help is enormous, but so is the potential to cause harm. The stakes are simply higher than in other industries. Clinicians are already stretched thin, and the last thing they need is technology that adds friction instead of removing it.

We help healthcare organizations identify AI applications that genuinely improve care delivery, operational efficiency, and patient outcomes, while navigating the regulatory and ethical considerations that are unique to this sector.

Healthcare AI Applications

  • Administrative burden reduction for clinical staff
  • Clinical documentation improvement and automation
  • Patient flow optimization and capacity planning
  • Revenue cycle and claims processing efficiency

Professional Services

Law firms, accounting firms, consulting firms, and other professional services organizations sell expertise. AI threatens to commoditize some of that expertise while creating opportunities to deliver more value, faster, to clients.

We help professional services firms figure out how to use AI to enhance their capabilities rather than be disrupted by it. The goal is to free up your professionals to do higher value work while AI handles the routine tasks that consume so much of their time.

Professional Services AI Opportunities

  • Document review and analysis automation
  • Research acceleration and knowledge management
  • Client communication and relationship management
  • Billing and matter management optimization

The Professional Services AI Challenge

Your business model depends on expertise. AI changes the economics of expertise. Here is how to navigate it:

Use AI to handle routine work, freeing professionals for complex, judgment intensive tasks.
Develop new service offerings that leverage AI capabilities your competitors have not figured out yet.
Train your team to work alongside AI tools effectively and ethically.
