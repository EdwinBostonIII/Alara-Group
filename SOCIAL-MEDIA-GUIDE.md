## Profile Images

### Logo Requirements

You will need to create or provide:

**Profile Picture / Logo**

- Square format (1:1 ratio)
- Minimum 400 by 400 pixels recommended
- Should be clear at small sizes
- Suggestion: Company wordmark or "AG" monogram

**LinkedIn Banner Image**

- 1128 by 191 pixels (recommended)
- Optional tagline overlay
- Brand colors: Slate (#0f172a), Ember orange (#ed7414), Cream (#fdfcfa)

**Facebook Cover Photo**

- 820 by 312 pixels on desktop
- 640 by 360 pixels on mobile
- Keep important content centered (edges may crop)

**X (Twitter) Header Image**

- 1500 by 500 pixels
- Keep important content centered (edges may crop on mobile)

### Design Suggestions

- Use the brand colors: deep slate, warm ember orange, cream backgrounds
- Keep designs clean and professional
- Consider including the tagline: "AI strategy that actually works"
- Fonts: Playfair Display for display text, Space Grotesk for accents

## Content Calendar Ideas

### Weekly Themes

**Monday: Industry Insights**

- AI developments specific to your target industries
- Case examples (anonymized) from client work
- Industry specific AI challenges and solutions

**Wednesday: Practical AI**

- Realistic assessments of AI tools and trends
- What works versus what is overhyped
- Questions leaders should ask about AI

**Friday: Thought Pieces**

- Strategy perspectives
- Leadership and change management in AI
- Commentary on AI news and announcements

### Post Ideas

**Educational:**

- "5 questions to ask before any AI investment"
- "Why most AI pilots fail (and how to avoid it)"
- "What your board actually needs to know about AI"
- "The difference between AI readiness and AI maturity"
- "How to evaluate an AI vendor (honest criteria)"

**Industry Specific:**

- "AI in insurance: Beyond claims automation"
- "What defense organizations get wrong about AI"
- "Healthcare AI that actually helps clinicians"
- "Professional services in the age of AI: adapt or decline"

**Contrarian Takes:**

- "Sometimes the right AI strategy is wait"
- "The case against AI pilots"
- "Most AI strategies fail before implementation. Here is why."

**Behind the Scenes:**

- Founder perspectives on AI trends
- What you are reading and learning
- Questions clients ask you most often

### Posting Frequency

- **LinkedIn:** 2 to 3 times per week
- **Facebook:** 1 to 2 times per week
- **X (Twitter):** 3 to 5 times per week (including replies and retweets)

## Posting Guidelines

### Tone

Professional but approachable. Confident without being arrogant. Direct without being blunt.

### What to Avoid

- Em dashes and excessive hyphens
- Jargon without explanation
- Vendor criticism by name
- Overpromising AI capabilities
- Generic motivational content

### What to Embrace

- Specific, concrete examples
- Honest assessments (even when unflattering to AI)
- Questions that provoke thought
- Sharing what you are learning
- Acknowledging complexity and nuance

## Account Security

1. Use strong, unique passwords for each platform
2. Enable two factor authentication on all accounts
3. Document who has admin access
4. Review connected apps periodically
5. Keep recovery email and phone number updated
