Our Story

We Have Been Where You Are

Here is the thing about AI consulting: most people doing it have never actually sat in your chair. They have never had to justify a technology investment to a skeptical board. They have never had to explain to their team why this change matters without causing panic.

We have. Between us, we have spent decades working inside organizations across insurance, defense, technology, healthcare, and professional services. We have seen what works, what fails, and most importantly, why.

We started Alara Group because we noticed something troubling: the gap between what AI promises and what businesses actually experience was getting wider. Industry research shows that while 88% of organizations now use AI in some form, only about 6% see significant bottom line impact. Too many smart leaders were either frozen by fear of getting it wrong or burned by vendors who sold them technology without a strategy to make it useful.

That is the gap we close. We translate the complex into the practical, and we do not leave until you can see the results.
