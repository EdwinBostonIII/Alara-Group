Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

Do you work with organizations of any size?

We work primarily with mid market and enterprise organizations. Our approach requires a certain level of organizational complexity to be valuable. That said, if you are a smaller company with ambitious AI goals, we are happy to have a conversation.

Do you build AI systems, or just advise?

We are strategists and advisors, not implementation engineers. We help you figure out what to build and how to build it successfully, but we do not write code or manage technical projects. We can, however, help you select and manage the teams that do.

What does a typical engagement look like?

It depends on your needs. Some clients start with a focused assessment that takes a few weeks. Others engage us for longer term advisory relationships. We will recommend an approach after understanding your situation.

How quickly can you start?

We maintain a deliberately small client base so we can give each engagement the attention it deserves. Depending on our current commitments, we can often begin exploratory conversations within a week or two.
